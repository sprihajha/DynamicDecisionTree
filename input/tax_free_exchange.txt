Are you planning on selling a property and buying another one (or vice versa) within 180 days?
--If Yes, Have you included the property you want to buy in a written document signed by the intermediary and delivered it to the property seller within 45 days of selling the initial property (you can typically identify up to 3 properties before you decide which to buy)?
----If Yes, Are both the properties you are buying and selling located in the U.S?
------If Yes, Are the properties you are buying and selling to be used productively either both in a trade or business or both for investment purposes?
--------If Yes, Are you receiving any assets in addition to the real estate you are purchasing as part of the exchange?
----------If Yes, Did you gain money on the overall exchange, i.e., do you have cash left over from the exchange after closing the deal on both properties?
------------If Yes, You will need to pay capital gains taxes on the immediate profits gained from the exchange.
